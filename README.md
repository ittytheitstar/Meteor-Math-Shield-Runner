# 🚀 Meteor Math Shield Runner

> **Play now → [https://ittytheitstar.github.io/Meteor-Math-Shield-Runner](https://ittytheitstar.github.io/Meteor-Math-Shield-Runner)**

A free, zero-install HTML5 maths game for New Zealand students aged 6–12. Steer your spaceship into the correct answer before the meteors strike!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose your **age group** (6–7, 8–9, or 10–12)
2. Pick a **game mode** (Quick Run, Zen Practice, or Daily Challenge)
3. Three meteors fall — each showing a possible answer
4. Steer your ship into the **correct answer** to deflect the meteor
5. **Wrong answers** drain your shield — keep it above zero!

### Controls

| Input | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Tap / Click | Move ship to that lane |
| ← / A key | Move left |
| → / D key | Move right |
| 1 / 2 / 3 keys | Jump to lane 1 / 2 / 3 |

---

## 👥 Profile System

Families with multiple children can each have their **own profile** with separate progress, XP, coins, and stats.

- **Guest mode** — Play immediately with default progress (saved to the device)
- **Named Profiles** — Add a name, choose an emoji icon, and set an optional 4-digit passcode
- **Switch profiles** at any time by tapping the profile indicator on the home screen

### Creating a Profile

1. Tap the profile button (👤 Guest) on the home screen
2. Tap **＋ Add Profile**
3. Enter a name, pick an icon, and optionally set a 4-digit passcode
4. Tap **Save** — your profile is active immediately

### Switching Profiles

1. Tap the profile indicator on the home screen
2. Tap a profile card to switch
3. If the profile has a passcode, enter it on the keypad

## 🎓 Curriculum Coverage

The game covers the New Zealand Mathematics Curriculum across three age bands:

### 🌱 Age 6–7 (Year 2–3)
- Addition & Subtraction (within 100)
- Number Patterns
- Money (coins & simple change)
- Geometry (basic shapes & perimeter)
- Statistics (reading simple data)

### ⭐ Age 8–9 (Year 4–5)
- Times Tables (2–12) & Division
- Fractions (halves, quarters, thirds, fifths)
- Algebra (missing number problems)
- Money (decimal amounts)
- Time (adding minutes/hours)
- Timetable reading
- Fraction equivalence

### 🔥 Age 10–12 (Year 6–8)
- Harder Multiplication & Division
- Fractions, Decimals & Percentages
- Algebra (two-step equations)
- Geometry (area & perimeter)
- Probability (simple & compound)
- Statistics (mean & mode)
- Composite area shapes
- Compound probability

## ⚡ Game Features

### 🔥 Fever Mode
Build a combo streak of 5+ correct answers to enter **Fever Mode**: double coins, reduced shield drain, and rainbow particles!

### 📅 Daily Challenge
A unique set of 10 questions generated fresh every day — the same for everyone. Compete with friends and family for today's high score.

### 🎯 Missions
Complete 13 progressive missions to unlock special ships and earn bonus XP & coins.

### 🛒 Upgrade Shop
Spend coins on 11 upgrades across three categories:
- **Survival**: Reinforced Shield, Shield Regen+, Second Chance, Shield Insulator
- **Speed & Score**: Combo Lock, XP Overdrive, Coin Magnet, Quick Tap
- **Math Help**: Eliminate!, Time Warp, Place Value Lens

### 🚀 Ships (Cosmetics)
8 ships to unlock through gameplay — from the default Scout to the legendary Taniwha.

### 💡 Teaching Tips
After every wrong answer, a teaching tip appears explaining the strategy and showing a worked example.

### 🌿 Te Reo Māori
Toggle the **Māori** language button on the home screen to translate key UI labels.

### 🌓 High Contrast Mode
An accessibility toggle for better visibility in bright environments.

## 📊 Adaptive Difficulty

The game automatically adjusts question difficulty using a sliding window of your last 10 answers:

- Accuracy > 85% → difficulty increases
- Accuracy < 65% → difficulty decreases
- Always stays within your age band's range

---

## 💾 Save Data

All progress is saved to your browser's **localStorage** — no account or internet required after the initial page load. Each profile stores:

- Total XP and level
- Best score per mode
- Total correct answers
- Coin balance and upgrades
- Unlocked ships
- Strand-by-strand accuracy stats
- Daily challenge results

## 🛠 Technical Details

- **Pure vanilla HTML5/CSS/JS** — no frameworks, no build tools
- **Canvas rendering** at 480×800px, CSS-scaled to any screen size
- **Web Audio API** for procedurally generated sound effects and ambient music
- **GitHub Pages** hosted — fork and deploy for free
